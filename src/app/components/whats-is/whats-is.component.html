What Is Devfest

DevFest is an annual distributed tech conference hosted by the Google Developer Groups (GDG) community. GDGs host these events around the globe. Through DevFest, you can continue to explore how Google's suite of developer tools can expand the impact of local tech professionals and developers like you around the world.

What to expect

Technical content

Worldwide events hosted by the Google Developer Groups community, featuring leading experts on Google technologies in areas like Cloud, Android, Firebase, Flutter, Machine Learning and so much more

Hands-On Sessions

Full-day workshops, hands-on experiences, hacking and debugging events, all centered on your favorite Google technologies.

Networking

Meet and connect with other developers in your community.
